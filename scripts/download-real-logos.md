## Note on Logo Usage

Most institutional logos are protected by trademark. For a production showcase:

**Option 1: Fair Use**
- Educational/informational use
- Cite sources we aggregate from
- No commercial misrepresentation

**Option 2: Text-based representation**
- Use official wordmarks/typography
- Match brand colors
- Clean, professional presentation

**Option 3: Request permission**
- Contact each organization
- Get written approval
- Proper attribution

**Current approach: Option 2** - Professional text-based logos with authentic brand colors.
